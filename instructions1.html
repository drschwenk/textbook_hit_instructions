Assigning Category Labels

To assign a category, first select the desired category by clicking one of the buttons on the left under 'Select Category.' Then click the question boxes highlighted on the page that are best described by that label. If there are any questions that don't fit into a category, they should be left unlabeled. After clicking on a box, its color will change to the match the category label you have selected. For speed, the categories are assigned hotkeys corresponding to their number (1-5).

Once you are done with a page, click the Submit Results button (or press the return key) to complete the HIT and send your results to Mechanical Turk. The next page will show the correct labeling of this text.
