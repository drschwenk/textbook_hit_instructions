Building Question Groups

Once you have labeled all parts of a given question, you'll mark these pieces as belonging to the same question by pressing the 'Save Question' button, or by pressing the hotkey 'q' on your keyboard. When a question has been saved, the transparency of its boxes will be reduced as a reminder. On this page, the short answer question "A." has been saved. In general, you should use the letters and numbers labeling the question as a guide when grouping the text into questions. The following pages will provide some grouping examples.
